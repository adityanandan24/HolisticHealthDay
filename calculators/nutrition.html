Understanding Nutritional Deficiencies

Proper nutrition is crucial for maintaining energy, mental clarity, and overall well-being. A deficiency in essential vitamins and minerals can lead to symptoms like fatigue, hair loss, and weakened immunity. Use this tool to identify potential deficiencies based on your symptoms and dietary habits.
