What is Heart Rate Variability (HRV)?

HRV measures the variation in time between heartbeats. It is an indicator of autonomic nervous system health and helps you understand how your body responds to stress, exercise, and relaxation.

Why HRV is Important?

A higher HRV score indicates better stress resilience and recovery capabilities. This assessment is useful for athletes, yoga practitioners, and individuals focused on stress management and overall wellness.
