Understanding Ayurveda Doshas

In Ayurveda, the three doshas—Vata, Pitta, and Kapha—are biological energies found throughout the human body and mind. Each dosha influences physical characteristics, mental tendencies, and overall well-being.

Why Knowing Your Dosha Matters

Understanding your dominant dosha can help you tailor your diet, exercise, and lifestyle to maintain balance and optimize health:

  • Vata: Focus on grounding practices and warm, nourishing foods.
  • Pitta: Incorporate cooling foods and relaxation techniques.
  • Kapha: Engage in stimulating exercises and lighter meals.
